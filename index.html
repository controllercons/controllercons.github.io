Controllercons

Controllercons were borne from a passion for video games and iconography. The free-to-use icon pack is available to any of you that want to pimp your website, add a bit of sparkle to a piece of print or jazz up your video title cards.

Controllercons is an ever-evolving project consisting of 16 icons, at launch, each with solid and outline variants. As you may have guessed, controllercons are based on beloved video game controllers from past and present. Each icon has been crafted to match the aesthetic of the controller whilst retaining legibility at different font sizes.

As the project grows so too will the variety of icons. This initial release includes some of the most well-known controllers from the past couple of decades of gaming but more will come!
